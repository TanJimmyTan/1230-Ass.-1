Dry line – New York – Bjarke Ingels East Side Coastal Resiliency

The Transformation This transformation involves the NYC lower manhattan waterfront to implement a 12km long infrastructure barrier. This barrier protects the city against flood water as well as improve eco-friendly ideas with green areas built on top. Another aspect that is improved is the general space and access people have to the waterfront. Intent Green areas allow for a larger variety of eco-friendly spaces that New York can possess, allowing a larger ‘Green’ presence overall. The flood barriers are to be implemented for the purpose of blocking flood water and restricting its access into the city.
